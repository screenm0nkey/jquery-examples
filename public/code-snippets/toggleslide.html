Environmental Approach

9web is committed to keeping its operations and supply chain as green as possible. We try hard to reduce our energy consumption, measure the residual carbon footprint (including the servers hosting our sites) and offset what we can't reduce through robust, verifiable carbon reduction projects in the third world.

In 2008 our carbon footprint was 3tCO2e, including office energy, server energy, staff travel & staff commuting. For more information please contact support@9web.co.uk
